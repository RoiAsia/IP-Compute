We are a 3rd year BSIT student at Phinma Araullo University, and we built a website that will compute the IP address of a network. Our website aims to provide a user-friendly platform for individuals to easily determine the IP address of a network. By simply entering the necessary information, such as the network ID and subnet mask, our website will generate the corresponding IP address. This tool will greatly benefit network administrators and IT professionals by efficiently managing and troubleshooting their networks.
